Timeline

InMōshn provides a kit that allows for an easy transformation of your traditional longboard or skateboard to an electric one! The kit is both adaptable to most boards and is affordable!

  • June 2016

    Team, Assemble!

    Sageevan, Darshak, and Prateek team up and think of products to develop that will help someone's daily life.

  • September 2016

    An Idea is Born

    The 3 engineers finalize on an idea to tackle. They decide on providing a solution to overcoming the pains of short commutes.

  • November 2016

    From Draft to CAD

    The team decides on a vehicle to build on and they draft a design for the electric longboard. The mechanical guy whips up a CAD model to bring the draft to (virtual) life

  • January 2017

    InMōshn is Alive!

    The team is named and a website (this one!) is put together to document the progress of InMōshn's first personal transportation vehicle. Check out our blog for more detailed updates on your next favourite way to get to work!

  • March 2016

    InMōshn on Video

    InMōshn finally completes building the board and launches a video to present it. Check it out on YouTube here!
